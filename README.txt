Import and Export stock statuses and quantities for WooCommerce products in CSV(Comma-Separated Values) format.

== Description ==

Product Stock Import and Export for WooCommerce plugin generates reports on the stock status (in stock / out of stock) and quantity of individual WooCommerce products. Reports can be downloaded in CSV (Comma-Separated Values) format, compatible with popular spreadsheet software. It also allows you to import CSV files to batch update your product stock data. This enables you to download a spreadsheet of your current inventory data, update it as required, and upload it back into WooCommerce.

== Installation ==

1. Click "Plugins" > "Add New" in the WordPress admin menu.
1. Search for "Product Stock Import and Export for WooCommerce".
1. Click "Install Now".
1. Click "Activate Plugin".

Alternatively, you can manually upload the plugin.
1. Upload the plugin folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the Plugins menu in WordPress.
1. Thats it! you can now use the plugin.
